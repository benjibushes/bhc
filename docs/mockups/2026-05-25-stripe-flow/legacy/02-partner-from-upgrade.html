Migration FAQ

What happens to my existing Payment Links?

They keep working until you manually switch your landing page to BHC Checkout. We'll guide you when you upgrade.

Can I switch back to legacy?

No — migration is one-way. Once you're on a tier, you stay on a tier. You can pause/cancel anytime, but you can't revert to the legacy 10% post-close model.

Will I lose my closed deal history?

No — all 23 of your closed Won + Lost referrals stay attached to your account.

One more step — Connect your bank to receive payouts

Why this step?

By federal law (KYC — Know Your Customer), any platform that handles payments must verify the operator's identity before sending money. Stripe (not BHC) holds your data.

What you'll need (~5 min):

  • Your legal name + SSN or EIN
  • Bank account routing + account number
  • Photo ID (driver's license)
  • Date of birth + address

Same flow PayPal, Square, and DoorDash use. You can pause and resume anytime.

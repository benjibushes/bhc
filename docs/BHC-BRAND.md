# BHC Brand — voice + mission

## Mission (shared)

**Connect every household to a ranch they trust.**

Use verbatim on `/founders`, homepage, /access footer, email signatures.
Not a tagline. The deepest reason we exist.

## Words we use

### Buyer voice (`/`, `/access`, `/qualify`, `/matched`, buyer emails)
- "your rancher" not "the platform"
- "lock your beef" not "complete checkout"
- "ranch-direct" not "B2B marketplace"
- "real food" not "premium product"
- "freezer-stocked" not "delivered order"
- First person ranchers when possible ("Jesse raises…")

### Rancher / infra voice (`/partner`, `/founders`, `/rancher`, rancher emails)
- "modern sales infrastructure" 
- "Stripe Connect direct charges"
- "buyer acquisition + qualification"
- "we bring buyers. you bring beef. keep the payouts."
- "stop chasing invoices, deposits, and customer support tickets."
- Stripe-style flat factual SaaS voice

### Operator voice (`/admin/*`)
- Short, dense, pipeline-oriented
- "$2,400 closed today · 3 deposits pending · 7 slots locked"
- Imperative when actionable ("Send Invoice", "Accept Slot")

## Words we DO NOT use

| Banned | Why | Use instead |
|---|---|---|
| "marketplace" | We're not Crowd Cow / Faire | "private network" or "ranch-direct" |
| "premium" | Cliched, dismissable | be specific: "grass-finished", "verified" |
| "luxury" | Wrong audience | omit |
| "10,000 families" | Fabricated stat | actual member count or omit |
| "The HERD" | Failed branding | "members", "the network" |
| "Private Network" capitalized as proper noun | Marketing-y | lowercase "private network" |
| Emoji in body copy | Slop signal | use sparingly — section dividers only |

## False-claim ban (audit grep regularly)

- No statistic without sourceable number
- No "trusted by X+ families" unless verified count is current
- No "5-star reviews" unless review count > 50
- No "FDA-approved" / "USDA-grass-finished" without per-rancher verification

## Mission integration checklist

When updating copy on any page, verify:
- [ ] Voice matches surface (table above)
- [ ] Banned words absent
- [ ] Mission stmt referenced or aligned-to where shared (homepage, /access, /founders, email signatures)
- [ ] No fabricated stats
- [ ] No emoji in body (section dividers only)
- [ ] Mobile preview reviewed
- [ ] On-brand within ±5% of equivalent surface

## Notes

- Dual voice is intentional — Stripe Checkout doesn't tell buyers about "payment infrastructure"; merchants don't see "Pay $X" UI. Same pattern here.
- Mission is the common thread regardless of voice.
- If unsure which voice: who is reading this surface most? Match their voice.

# BHC Marketing Engine + Video Production Plan

> Applied via `marketing-psychology` skill (Cialdini + behavioral econ + JTBD).
> Sources: docs/BHC.md (canon) · docs/VISION.md · docs/BUSINESS-MODEL.md · `.claude/skills/bhc-marketing/`.

**Goal:** Convert "platform launched + 16 ranchers operational" into a self-sustaining engine. Buyer side: 500 signups/month organic + paid. Rancher side: 30 verified ranchers by month 2. Brand side: 3 paying partners by month 3.

**Architecture:** Three-track engine running parallel:
1. **Educational content** (asymmetric trust-building — buyers + ranchers learn before they buy)
2. **Founder narrative** (mimetic desire + authority + unity — Ben's voice carries platform identity)
3. **Conversion ladders** (each piece moves prospects one step toward signup/founder/partnership)

Each video, post, email, ad is **deliberately mapped to a psychology model** + a funnel stage + a measurable conversion event.

---

## North Star Metric

**Closed Won deals per month.** Everything ladders to this. Not signups, not views, not impressions — closed deals.

Why: deals = ranchers earning + buyers fed + commission + flywheel input. Vanity metrics (followers, opens) report leading indicators but the North Star keeps every decision aligned.

## What kills this plan

Per inversion principle — list what would guarantee failure + avoid:

1. **Shooting 12 videos before publishing any.** Ship pillar 1 in week 1, iterate.
2. **Polishing production over publishing cadence.** A B+ video published this week beats an A+ video published in 3 months.
3. **Generic "we sell premium beef" messaging.** Every asset must tie to a specific JTBD + a specific audience segment.
4. **Skipping the rancher testimonials.** Buyer-side videos are easier — but rancher testimonials drive the supply growth that everything else depends on.
5. **Going broad on channels.** Pick 3 channels in Tier 1, master them, then expand. IG + email + YouTube wins.
6. **Letting metrics distract from North Star.** Closed Won deals. Everything else is leading indicator.

## Why this plan works

Each video does double duty: educates the audience while ALSO seeding a specific psychology trigger that makes the next conversion easier. Mimetic desire from rancher testimonials → social proof on the homepage → authority bias from verification walk-through → reciprocity from free cuts guide → foot-in-the-door via 60-second quiz → endowment via numbered Founder badge.

Compounding effects + flywheel: every video published = more SEO surface + more email send context + more cold-outreach attachment + more retargeting fuel. The 12 videos are not 12 one-shots — they're 12 reusable assets that each fire across 5+ channels.

Founder-voice consistency is the single biggest moat. Anyone can copy a marketplace. Nobody can copy Ben's voice + the specific ranchers in the network + the public ledger commitment. The marketing engine reinforces the moat by making the voice ubiquitous.

The plan is shippable on iPhone budget + 6 weeks of founder time. No external agency. No paid course. No specialty hire.

<a name="inversion"></a>
## 13. WHAT KILLS THIS PLAN (Inversion)

Per the inversion principle — what would guarantee failure:

1. **Quality over publishing cadence.** B+ video today > A+ video in 3 weeks.
2. **Reactive vs proactive sales motion.** Don't wait for inbound. Cold-message daily.
3. **Skipping rancher testimonials.** Buyer-side easier, but rancher-side drives supply growth that everything depends on.
4. **Over-promising backers.** Public ledger = transparency. Don't make claims you can't deliver.
5. **Letting metrics distract from North Star.** Closed Won is the metric. Followers + views = leading indicators only.
6. **Burning out.** 90 days = a sprint. Pace within that.
7. **Founder bottleneck.** Content + responses are founder-only. Block 2-3 hours/day for production or it crowds out.
8. **Skipping Stripe Connect prep.** Day 60+ start thinking Phase 1 engineering. By Day 90 have a builder lined up.

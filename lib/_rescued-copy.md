## Voice notes for the rebuild

- Lowercase "Hey {firstName}," opener
- "Quick update" framing — defuses sales pressure
- First-person founder presence ("I'm on the road", "I'm meeting")
- Call to action that's NOT a button — it's a reply or a forward
- Mission line in italics or pullquote: *"We're gonna take back American
  ranching and agriculture"* — this is the soul of the brand
- No corporate footer ("Private Network for American Ranch Beef") — just
  "— Benjamin" or "— Benjamin, Founder"
- Asks for help (forward to a rancher) — the buyer becomes a recruiter

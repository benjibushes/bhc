# partner-dm-templates

**Audience:** existing partner ranchers (Sackett, High Lonesome, AU Beef, etc.)
**Channel:** DM / SMS / casual email (whichever channel they already reply on)
**Voice:** rancher-to-founder, lowercase, no corporate spin, no asks unless earned

---

## TEMPLATE A — "we launched, here's your map pin"

**Use when:** rancher is verified + live, you want to nudge them to share the map link.

```
hey {firstName} — quick one.

we went live with the public map today. you're on it — verified pin, your page, your pricing, your photos. live at buyhalfcow.com/map.

if you want to share it anywhere — your IG, your shopify newsletter, a text to your customer list — your direct link is buyhalfcow.com/ranchers/{slug}. anyone who lands there and books goes straight through your dashboard, your close, your 90%.

no pressure to share. just wanted you to see it's live and looking sharp.

— Ben
```

*Word count: 102*

---

## TEMPLATE B — "first deal closed via platform, here's the case study"

**Use when:** a rancher closes their first deal through the network. Send within 48 hours.

```
{firstName} — your first close hit the dashboard today. congrats.

i wrote it up as a public case study at buyhalfcow.com/ranchers/{slug} — pulled your numbers, the buyer's state, the cuts they bought, the timeline from intro to close. let me know if anything looks off or you want a sentence cut.

i'd love to share it on twitter + the founder letter going out friday. quick yes/no — if you'd rather we keep it private for now, just say the word and we hold.

either way: first close on the network. that's a real number now.

— Ben
```

*Word count: 117*

---

## TEMPLATE C — "we're opening to brand partners, want to be early?"

**Use when:** rolling out brand-partner intros to ranchers who might want sponsored placement on their page or in their newsletter.

```
hey {firstName} — heads up before this hits the broader list.

we're opening a brand-partner program — coolers, knives, cutting boards, regen supplements, ranching media. three tiers, gated carefully. brands pay us, not you. but when a brand sponsors something tied to your page or your story, you get first refusal + a cut.

before i open it to brands publicly i wanted to ask the verified ranchers: any brand you already use + recommend that you'd want me to bring on first? i'll reach out to them with your name attached.

reply with a name or a "nope, not for me" — either works.

— Ben
```

*Word count: 118*

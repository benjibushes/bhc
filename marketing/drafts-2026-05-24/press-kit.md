## THE STORY

BuyHalfCow is a marketplace and software platform that connects families directly to verified small-scale ranchers in their own state, replacing the six-middleman supply chain that delivers most grocery beef. Families take a 90-second quiz to describe what they want — quarter, half, or whole — and the network routes them to a screened ranch within driving distance. The rancher closes the deal directly with the family and keeps the customer relationship. BuyHalfCow takes a 10% commission, charged only after a sale closes.

The platform launched its public marketplace in early 2026 and has verified seven ranches across five states, with 1,309 buyers in its pipeline as of May 2026. The company is opening a "Founding Herd" capital campaign on May 24, 2026, with 100 numbered backer spots ranging from $100 to $15,000, structured as perks-only — no equity, no SAFE, no securities. Fifteen percent of net annual profit is contractually committed to a rancher dividend pool, distributed by GMV contribution. An additional five percent funds grants to small USDA-inspected processors and regenerative grazing transition.

Founder Ben Beauchman started BuyHalfCow after watching two ranching families he knew personally lose their direct-sales businesses to a combination of feedlot pricing pressure and the closure of their regional processor. The company is headquartered remotely, builds in public, and publishes a quarterly open expense ledger to all backers and ranchers.
